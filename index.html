Country Music is Dying... Fast

Can today's "country music" really be classified as country? That's what I'm here to talk about in Country Music is Dying... Fast. Today's "country music" isn't even close to the good old traditional country music that it tries to mimic. The values, sounds, and meaning that used to exist in country music is no longer present. So then how would today's music even be able to call itself country. In my opinion, it can't. Now we have pop, rock, folk, etc., and it seems like country is nowhere to be found. So how much longer does it have?

The Mona Lisa of Video Games

What is the Mona Lisa of video games? How are video games considered art? These are questions that we dive into when discussing one of our favorite hobbies and pastimes. In our podcast, we discuss the two games The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild and The Outer Wilds. We delve into what makes these games amazing and how they utilize elements of gameplay as a medium, so the user can have an experience that can be called an artform.
